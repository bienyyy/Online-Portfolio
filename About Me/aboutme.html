ALL ABOUT ME

Hi, I’m Bien Dranreb Gablines, the eldest son in the family and also the first grandchild. I'm an introvert, but it depends on the bond I build with others. I don't really like heavy crowds, socializing, or interacting with people much because of how strict my grandmother was back then. This made me prefer being alone and occupying myself with what I have in my room. Right now, I’m a first-year college student taking up Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering at University of Bohol. My goal in life is to succeed in my field while also figuring out what happiness truly means to me.

Growing up, I was always a curious kid, fascinated by how things worked. I love reading books, especially those that spark my interest, such as science-related books, drama, and manga, e.g., Demon Slayer. I also love solving problems and getting to the root of things because I believe everything happens for a reason. My childhood wasn’t exactly easy, but I made the most of it, learned a lot along the way, and let those experiences shape who I am today.

Now that I’m 18 years old, I focus on improving myself, learning from past mistakes, and gaining new life experiences. I understood how cruel life can be at a young age, leading me to experience early depression. I've struggled ever since, but it hasn't stopped me from becoming the person I am today and striving to be even better. One of my biggest passions is speedcubing, where I developed a deep interest in twisty puzzles. I'm proud to be part of an amazing global community, where solving these puzzles feels like free therapy. Meeting others who share the same passion is truly inspiring, especially knowing that it all started with the brilliant mind of Erno Rubik.

Additionally, I enjoy playing MOBA games like Mobile Legends: Bang Bang and Pokémon Unite. I also love solving riddles, tricky puzzles, and anything that challenges my mind. More importantly, I love helping people whenever I can, because even small acts of kindness can make a big difference.

As Erno Rubik once said:

"If you are curious, you'll find the puzzles around you. If you are determined, you will solve them."

Abode

with a simple meaning to it.
We can all say it's a place 
that we like to call "the safe place."

Some may say it's the people 
who make us feel at "home,"
others would say it's our family

Dark as Cold

laying down in my bed, a dark empty room 
glarring upon the sealings, seeing pitch darkness

come to think of it, sharing our problems to others 
was a waste of time, waste of energy
